# YHack 2026 - Key Info

## Dates & Times
- **Event**: March 28-29, 2026
- **Hacking starts**: Saturday March 28, 11:00 AM
- **Hacking stops**: Sunday March 29, 11:00 AM
- **Video submission**: 11:00 AM - 11:30 AM Sunday
- **Judging**: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM Sunday (Kline Tower 205)
- **Closing Ceremony + Awards**: 3:00 PM Sunday (OC Marsh)

## Locations
- **OC Marsh Lecture Hall** (Yale Science Building) - 260 Whitney Ave - Main hub, registration, ceremonies, meals
- **Davies Auditorium** (Becton Center) - 15 Prospect Street - Overflow check-in
- **Kline Tower** - 219 Prospect Street - Sponsor booths, hacking space, workshops, judging (Room 205)
- **17 Hillhouse Avenue** (HLH17) - More working space (Rooms 03, 05, 111, 113, 115)

## Rules
1. All team members must be checked in
2. At least one member must demo in person on Sunday
3. All coding/building must happen during hacking hours (Sat 11AM - Sun 11AM)
4. Max 4 people per team
5. They WILL check GitHub repos to verify work was done during the hackathon
